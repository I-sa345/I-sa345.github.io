Flowers Festival

The Flowers Festival, or, Feria de las Flores is an incredible holiday that is celebrated in different dates on August. Is special for the paisas and all the people in Colombia because, since 1923, celebrates the culture and the extreme biodiversity of this department. It has multiple parades, such as the Vintage Cars parade, or the Silleteros parade. If you want to know the entire schedule and the brand new parades, you can visit this link.
