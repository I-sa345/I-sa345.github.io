How to embed a Youtube video on a web page:

1) In first place, go to Youtube and type in your video or, as in my case, channel of preference

2) Once you have searched a channel or a video, you will proceed selecting the video that you want to use in your web page

3) Look to the buttons over the description of the video and then select the "Share" button

4) Once you have done that, a series of options will display. You will select the one with the <___>

5) Then, you will have to copy the piece of code that its indicated using the option given by the page

6) We are almost done, our last thing to do is to incorporate the piece of code that the page gave us into our code. Remember to add the mandatory disclaimer in the tag
